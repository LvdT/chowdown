---

layout: recipe
title: "Pasta"
tags: pasta

ingredients:
- 1 L water
- 100 g Pasta
- 10 g salt

directions:
- Pour the water into a soup pot, bring to boil
- Add the salt
- Cook for the appropriate amount of time (as directed)
- Strain the pasta

tips:
- Optionally save some of the cooking water for thickening sauces
