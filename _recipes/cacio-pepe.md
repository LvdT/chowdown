---

layout: recipe
title: "Cacio e Pepe"
tags: pasta, sauce

ingredients:
- 35 g grated pecorino
- 2 g pepper

directions:
- Cook and drain the pasta, save around 40 mL of the water
- Add the cooking water to the cheese and whip until creamy
- Mix the pasta with the sauce and plate
- Powder with pepper

---
