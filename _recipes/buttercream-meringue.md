---

layout: recipe
title: "Buttercream (Meringue)"
tags: cream

ingredients:
- 120 g butter

directions:
- Make sure the butter is at room temperature and whip it until softened
- Fold the butter into the meringue

components:
- Italian Meringue
- Swiss Meringue

---

Only use one of the three components, depending on which you prefer.
